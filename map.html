Health Data Mining

Global Scale
Through all the processes of data gathering, storage and management, many different equipment and materials are needed to convert the bio-samples into virtual data. Due to the rapid growing demand on health data monitoring and genetic data analysis, many companies from all over the world are now providing equipment for the US market that is used in different steps.
